RARE BEAUTY- #berare

About the Campaigns

Rare Beauty’s marketing campaigns have been instrumental in reshaping how beauty brands approach inclusivity, self-love, and authenticity. Across its various campaigns, Rare Beauty focuses on several key messages and values that have significantly impacted the beauty industry.

More Than Makeup

Rare Beauty is redefining beauty standards by promoting authenticity, self-acceptance, and mental well-being. The brand believes that beauty is not about hiding imperfections, but embracing what makes each person unique. Rare Beauty’s campaigns center around real people, real stories, and real emotions — creating a movement that challenges traditional norms and uplifts diverse voices.

Make a Rare Impact

Rare Beauty donates 1% of all sales to the Rare Impact Fund, which aims to increase access to mental health services for young people globally. This initiative reflects the brand's commitment to healing both inside and out.

Comfort Club

Comfort Club is Rare Beauty’s online self-care space, offering guided meditation, breathing exercises, and gentle movement practices like yoga. It’s part of the brand’s ongoing commitment to mental wellness — creating a safe, supportive environment that encourages people to slow down, check in with themselves, and prioritize their emotional well-being.

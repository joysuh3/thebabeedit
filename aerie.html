AERIE- #aeriereal

About the Campaign

The #AerieREAL campaign was launched by Aerie (American Eagle’s lingerie and lifestyle brand) in 2014 with the mission to redefine beauty standards in the fashion and intimates industry. The "Real Me" line, an extension of this campaign, focuses on comfort, confidence, and authenticity.

  • No Retouching

    Aerie made a bold promise to stop retouching models in their ads. They proudly show stretch marks, scars, cellulite, and natural features.

  • Diverse Representation

    The campaign highlights models of all body types, abilities, ethnicities, and gender identities — including people with chronic illnesses, disabilities, and varying skin tones.

  • The “Real Me” Collection

    A soft, lightweight fabric line designed to feel like a second skin — it’s about both physical comfort and emotional confidence.

  • REAL Voices & Aerie Changemakers

    Aerie uplifts community leaders and everyday people with inspiring stories, giving them a platform to promote self-love, mental health, and body confidence.

  • Philanthropy

    Through the Aerie Real Foundation, the brand supports nonprofits focused on youth empowerment, education, and wellness.

Jennifer Foyle, President and Executive Creative Director of AE & Aerie — the visionary behind the #AerieREAL movement.

The Impact

The #AerieREAL campaign has made a significant mark on the fashion and advertising industries by encouraging inclusivity and authenticity. Its transparent approach to representation has sparked conversations and influenced other brands to follow suit.

  • Positive Customer Response

    Many customers expressed deeper trust and appreciation for the brand’s authenticity, leading to increased brand loyalty and engagement.

  • Industry Recognition

    Aerie received multiple awards and media praise for their bold stance on real beauty and body positivity.

  • Social Media Movement

    The campaign inspired thousands to share their unfiltered stories and photos using the #AerieREAL hashtag, fostering a strong online community.

  • Sales Growth

    Aerie experienced a notable rise in sales, demonstrating that ethical marketing and inclusivity can also drive business success.
